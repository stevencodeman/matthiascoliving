Our Mission

At Matthias Co-Living, we are committed to providing a supportive environment where individuals can recover with dignity and peace of mind. Our Recuperative Care program, also known as medical respite care, ensures that those who no longer require hospital care have access to a safe, clean, and nurturing place to heal.

Our Story

Founded in Riverside, CA, Matthias Co-Living was created to help people rebuild their lives with structure, support, and compassion. We believe everyone deserves a fresh start.
