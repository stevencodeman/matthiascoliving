Our Mission

At Matthias Co-Living, we are committed to providing a safe, supportive, and empowering environment for individuals on their recovery journey. Our community is built on respect, growth, and hope.

Our Story

Founded in Riverside, CA, Matthias Co-Living was created to help people rebuild their lives with structure, support, and compassion. We believe everyone deserves a fresh start.
