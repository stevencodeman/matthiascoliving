At Path Of Matthias Co-Living, we are committed to providing a supportive environment where individuals can recover with dignity and peace of mind. Our Recuperative Care program, also known as medical respite care, ensures that those who no longer require hospital care have access to a safe, clean, and nurturing place to heal.

Our Mission

To help individuals recover safely, prevent unnecessary hospital readmissions, and provide the care and support needed for a full recovery.

What We Offer

  • Thoughtfully Designed Living Spaces: Fully furnished, safe, and tranquil homes with access to clean water, heating, air conditioning, and stable utilities.
  • Comprehensive Support Services: Our trained staff provides medication reminders, assistance with wound care, and coordination for follow-up medical appointments.
  • Nutritious Meals & Daily Support: Residents receive health-conscious meals and assistance with activities of daily living to support their recovery journey.
  • A Healing Environment: Our homes uphold a quiet, drug- and alcohol-free atmosphere, fostering an ideal space for rest and recovery.

Who We Serve

We proudly welcome individuals referred by healthcare providers who require a stable and supportive place to recover after a hospital stay or medical treatment. These homes are single-family residences, integrated within their communities to provide a sense of normalcy and belonging.

Our Commitment to Excellence

At Path Of Matthias Co-Living, we uphold the highest standards of professionalism and compassion. By creating environments designed for healing, we ensure that each individual receives the care and respect they deserve as they transition toward stability and freedom.
